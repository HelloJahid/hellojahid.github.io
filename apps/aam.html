CSIRO TraNSIT OD Explorer

An experimental tool for exploring Australian road freight data from the CSIRO TraNSIT Benchmarking platform. Analyse origin–destination freight flows at the LGA level across commodities, costs, emissions, and industries.

📊 TraNSIT Dashboard — Enter any Origin → Destination LGA pair to retrieve detailed freight metrics including annual tonnes, transport costs, CO₂ emissions, trip distance, freight value, and industry breakdowns with charts.
🔬 Commodity OD Rankings — Rank all freight corridors nationally or from a single origin by individual commodity (e.g. Beer, Wheat, Steel). Filter by commodity, sector, or industry group.
