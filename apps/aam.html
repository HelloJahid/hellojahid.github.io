Advanced Air Mobility — Research Tools

AAM Project

Interactive tools supporting passenger and freight demand characterisation, and corridor analysis for zero-emissions aviation research in Australia.

Active Applications

🧭

Localis SampleData OD Explorer

An interactive analytical tool for exploring device-level mobility data to understand passenger movement patterns across Victoria. Supports origin–destination analysis at the LGA level for Advanced Air Mobility corridor planning.

📍 OD Passenger Flows — Analyse origin–destination passenger flows derived from large-scale mobility data and explore top travel corridors by trip frequency and device counts.
🕐 Temporal Patterns — Examine hourly distributions and weekday versus weekend travel trends to characterise regional travel demand behaviour.
📏 Distance-Based Corridor Screening — Evaluate distance-based travel behaviour to identify long-distance corridors (≥70 km) suitable for AAM feasibility analysis.
🔔 App Update (27 Mar 2026): Initial release — device-level OD explorer for Victoria passenger mobility data.

CSIRO TraNSIT OD Explorer

An experimental tool for exploring Australian road freight data from the CSIRO TraNSIT Benchmarking platform. Analyse origin–destination freight flows at the LGA level across commodities, costs, emissions, and industries.

📊 TraNSIT Dashboard — Enter any Origin → Destination LGA pair to retrieve detailed freight metrics including annual tonnes, transport costs, CO₂ emissions, trip distance, freight value, and industry breakdowns with charts.
🔬 Commodity OD Rankings — Rank all freight corridors nationally or from a single origin by individual commodity (e.g. Beer, Wheat, Steel). Filter by commodity, sector, or industry group.
🔔 App Update (19 Mar 2025): More visualisation added to the app based on Commodity Filter Insights.
