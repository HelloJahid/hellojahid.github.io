GroundingCarDD:

Text-Guided Multimodal Phrase Grounding for Car Damage Recognition

▶ RMIT University
Paper Live Demo Code Dataset

Abstract

GroundingCarDD, a pre-training model, combines car damage detection with "phrase grounding" to enhance visual representations. This innovative approach enables the model to learn from detection data as well as data associating words with damage in car parts. Consequently, it improves accuracy while also leveraging extensive image-text datasets for enhanced semantic understanding. This empowers GroundingCarDD to identify damage even in challenging scenarios. By introducing an open vocabulary car damage detector, GroundingCarDD effectively tackles the challenges associated with car features and the automotive environment. The model exhibits superior capability in distinguishing between car damages, car features, and environmental elements, thereby reducing the false positive rate.





Model

GroundingCarDD is a phrase grounding model that bridges the gap between visual and textual information. By accurately aligning textual descriptions of car damage with specific image regions, it offers a robust solution for precise damage assessment. This innovative approach empowers various applications, including insurance claims processing, automotive repair, and autonomous vehicle safety.:
